eCommerce

eCommerce has become a sales channel that noone can ignore. If you wish to compete effectively in today's market, your online sales channel can not be neglected.

Regardless of whether you are an eCommerce pure-play, a manufacturer of specialized goods or a traditional trading business, the online sales channel has developed into an important factor for the increase of brand awareness, customer loyalty and customer satisfaction.

Through increased efficiency in order processing as well as the continuous and effective optimization of the buying experience, an efficiently implemented eCommerce presence can be an important factor for cost savings and customer loyalty. The 24/7 availability as well as the improved customer service are an essential component in the improvement of customer satisfaction.

Innovation Brigade creates specialized solutions which transform your eCommerce strategy into a competitive advantage. Effectively implemented eCommerce allows you to increase your revenue, improve long term customer loyalty, acquire new customers and increase your brand awareness.

Our platform provides you with a large number real-time reporting capabilities which help you discover your most profitable customers and products, your inventory levels and other data which is relevant to the performance of your business. Innovation Brigade eCommerce is designed to be flexible and extensible. Whether you wish to add specialized product views, new payment methods, automatically import product data or export order data, by leveraging our architecture, Innovation Brigade eCommerce allows you to implement such changes quickly and with high quality.

If you have complex needs and wish to have them analyzed by us, contact us with your requirements and we will analyze their impact and generate an implementation quote for you. Development

When evaluating software for a particular purpose, it is often the case that existing solutions almost cover your specific needs. It is this „almost“ which can cause significant problems and costs when implementing new software in your organization, since it may require altering your business processes in order to be compatible with the way the software process requires.

In any complex organization, existing business processes are the result of conscious and unconscious optimization processes over time. Since every company works slightly differently, standard software can only suit your business processes to a certain degree.

The goal must thus be to close this gap between desire and reality. Rather than changing your business processes, Innovation Brigade offers you development services which adapts software to your specific needs.

Whether you need a custom application which is entirely specific to you or changes to an existing solution in order to adapt it your needs, Innovation Brigade has the know-how and skills to provide you with a system which works according to your specific requirements. Doing so, allows your organization to retain it's successful processes and keep utilizing the skills upon which your business depends and which your customers have come to expect and ensures that you retain the operational efficiency you have acquired over the years. Utilize the efficiency you have acquired over the years and inspire your customers with improved services.
